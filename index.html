About Me

I'm İbrahim Gültekin, a civil engineering student graduating in 2025 from Yaşar University, and the founder of Neo Gültekin. Born and raised in Karşıyaka, İzmir, I've been immersed in the construction industry since elementary school through my family's 30+ years of experience in the field.

What drives me isn't just carrying forward a family legacy—it's the opportunity to shape city skylines and transform how people experience their living spaces. Turkey's construction sector presents remarkable opportunities for innovation, and I'm passionate about bringing fresh perspectives that blend quality craftsmanship with modern technology.

My approach is simple: always strive to be the best in the region, focusing on quality over quantity. Whether it's through my academic work in flood prevention design or my entrepreneurial ventures, I believe in creating solutions that make a lasting impact.
